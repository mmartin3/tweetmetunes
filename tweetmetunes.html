Welcome to the Tweet Me Tunes portal!

Tweet Me Tunes is a Twitter music recommendation bot under development for Professor Zacharski's Data Mining and Information Retrieval class at the University of Mary Washington.

Tweet Me Tunes is also powered by Last.fm and YouTube. Get the source code at GitHub.

Queries must be formatted as @replies...

@tweetmetunes like search query

...or as direct messages.

d tweetmetunes like search query

Direct messages are more reliable than @replies, but @tweetmetunes must be following you on Twitter to send one. Just start following the bot and it will follow you back automatically.

Enter the name of an artist and/or track in the search box to the right and click "Tweet me tunes!" to have your query tweet formatted for you.
